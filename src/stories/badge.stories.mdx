<Preview>

### Colors
Badges can be colorized by adding `color` tag to the component.
`gray` is the default badge color.
<Preview>
  <Badge color="gray">Gray Badge</Badge>
  <Badge color="blue">Blue Badge</Badge>
  <Badge color="green">Green Badge</Badge>
  <Badge color="yellow">Yellow Badge</Badge>
  <Badge color="orange">Orange Badge</Badge>
  <Badge color="red">Red Badge</Badge>
</Preview>

### Sizes
Badges can be sized by adding the `size` tag to the component. Sizes come in
`xs`, `sm`, `md`, `lg`, `xl`.  `xs` is the default size.
<Preview>
  <Badge>XS Badge</Badge>
  <Badge size="sm">Small Badge</Badge>
  <Badge size="md">Regular Badge</Badge>
  <Badge size="lg">Large Badge</Badge>
  <Badge size="xl">XL Badge</Badge>
</Preview>
