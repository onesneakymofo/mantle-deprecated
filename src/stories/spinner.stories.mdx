### Sizes
Spinners can be sized by adding the `size` tag to the component. Sizes come in
`xs`, `sm`, `md`, `lg`, `xl`, `2xl`.  No size specified defaults to `md`.

<Preview>
    <Spinner size="xs" />
    <Spinner size="sm" />
    <Spinner size="md" />
    <Spinner size="lg" />
    <Spinner size="xl" />
    <Spinner size="2xl" />
</Preview>

### Colors
Spinners can be colorized by adding the `color` tag to the component. Colors come in
`blue`, `white`, or `gray`.  No color specified defaults to `blue`.
